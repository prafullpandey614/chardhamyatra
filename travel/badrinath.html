How to reach ?

Badrinath is located in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand and is easily accessible from the major cities, towns, and states of the country. This is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites and a prominent part of Char Dham Yatra. Badrinath city has easy access to all the modes of transportation: air, train, and road. This sacred town is flocked by a large number of pilgrims every year during Char Dham Yatra. If someone is looking for information about how to reach Badrinath, then here we have provided comprehensive details about the route and modes of transportation. The best option to reach Badrinath is by road or train. Besides this, pilgrims can choose to visit Badrinath by helicopter from Delhi and Dehradun.
